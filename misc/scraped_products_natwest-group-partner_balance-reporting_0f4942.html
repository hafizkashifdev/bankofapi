Direct Access Balance Reporting

Overview
Documentation

If you’re a commercial or corporate customer, our Balance Reporting service can give you regular updates on your account balances.

We can provide you with automatic notifications, through your internal systems, every 15 minutes (whenever there is a change in your account balances).

You can use this service alongside our Direct Access Accounts & Transactions API, which can give you direct connectivity to your NatWest, Royal Bank of Scotland or Ulster Bank account and transaction data through your own systems.

Next Steps – Apply for live access

Once you’ve successfully tested the Balance Reporting service in our sandbox, you can apply for live access. Please note that this process should be completed by someone in your organisation who has the authority to make a commitment on behalf of your business and is a signatory on your organisation’s bank account. Once you’ve submitted your application, we’ll review it and get back to you within 5 working days.

Read some of our most frequently asked questions

Who do we need to involve to complete the customer consent journey?

A technical employee will need to configure the communication channels and provide a link to someone with access to your company’s NatWest accounts to login to select the account and provide consent. For details about the domain to use when constructing the authentication request URL, see this documentation.

Please note that to connect our services to your systems, you’ll need the support of a developer with integration experience.

Before applying for live access, your developer will need to successfully test the Direct Access service in our sandbox. This is to ensure you have the appropriate technical expertise to integrate our service with your systems

What tools do I need for the technical onboarding process?

You’ll need access to OpenSSL and Java Key Tool.

Is my company eligible?

You can gain direct access to our Direct Access services if you meet the following criteria;

  • Hold NatWest UK accounts
  • Meets MiFID criteria for Professional Client or Eligible Counterparty; OR
  • Average turnover for last 3 years ≥ £6.5m; OR
  • Average total assets for the last 3 years ≥ £3.26m; OR
  • Is part of a Group connection that meets turnover or assets test
